## About ASIACOMB
ASIACOMB is a biennial conference that fosters international collaboration by strengthening research links across regions and providing a high-level forum for ideas in combinatorics.   

The first conference, ASIACOMB26, will be held in Daejeon, South Korea from August 24 to 28, 2026.


## Important Dates

Abstract submission deadline: March 22, 2026   
Notification of acceptance: May 10, 2026   
Proceedings camera ready: May 30, 2026   
Early registration deadline: June 14, 2026   
Late registration deadline: August 14, 2026   
Conference: August 24-28, 2026   
 
Nominations for the European Prize in Combinatorics: ToDo
