## Information about the Venue

The conference will be held at the Daejeon Convention Center (DCC) in Daejeon, South Korea.
![View of DCC](/assets/images/DCC.jpg)


The DCC is located at 107 Expo-ro, Yuseong-gu, Daejeon, South Korea. For more information about the venue, please visit the [DCC official website](https://www.dcckorea.or.kr/eng/index.do).


### Getting to Daejeon

After arriving at Incheon International Airport (ICN), you can take one of the following options to reach Daejeon:

- **KTX Train**: Take the Airport Railroad Express (AREX) from Incheon Airport to Seoul Station, then transfer to the KTX train to Daejeon Station (Seodaejeon station for some trains). The total travel time is approximately 2.5 to 3 hours. 
- **Express Bus**: There are direct express buses from Incheon Airport to Daejeon. There are several stations in Daejeon and Daejeon Government Complex (대전청사) is recommended. The travel time is approximately 3 to 4 hours, depending on traffic conditions.


### Getting to DCC
- **Taxi**: Anywhere in Daejeon, you can take a taxi to DCC. Depending on your starting location, the fare is usually between 5,000 to 15,000 KRW. Kakao Taxi is a popular app for hailing taxis in South Korea and Uber is also available to use regular taxis. If taxi drivers have difficulty finding the location, you can show them the name in Korean: 대전컨벤션센터.
- **Public Transportation**: Daejeon has one subway line (Line 1) and several bus routes that connect various parts of the city. You can use a reloadable T-money card for subway and buses; recharge at convenience stores or station kiosks. The fare is typically around 1,300 to 1,700 KRW for a single ride and if you use T-money, then transfer within 30 minutes is free.
    - **From Daejeon Station**: Bus No. 707 to DCC (approx. 1 hour) or take a subway to Government Complex Daejeon Station and then transfer to Bus No. 618 (approx. 40 minutes).
    - **From Bus Station at Daejeon Government Complex (대전청사)**: Bus No. 911 or 618 to DCC (approx. 20 minutes).
