## Information about the Venue

The conference will be held at the Daejeon Convention Center (DCC) in Daejeon, South Korea.
The DCC is located at 107 Expo-ro, Yuseong-gu, Daejeon, South Korea. For more information about the venue, please visit the [DCC official website](https://www.dcckorea.or.kr/eng/index.do).


### Getting to Daejeon

After arriving at Incheon International Airport (ICN), you can take one of the following options to reach Daejeon:

- **KTX Train**: Take the Airport Railroad Express (AREX) from Incheon Airport to Seoul Station, then transfer to the KTX train to Daejeon Station (Seodaejeon station for some trains). The total travel time is approximately 2.5 to 3 hours.
- **Express Bus**: There are direct express buses from Incheon Airport to Daejeon. The travel time is approximately 3 to 4 hours, depending on traffic conditions.
